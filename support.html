Frequently asked

How do I manage or cancel my Interval Pro subscription?
Open the Settings app on your device → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Interval Pro.

Does Interval work offline?
Yes. All of your tasks and notes are stored on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync in Interval's Settings, your data also syncs across your devices via your personal iCloud account.

How do I delete my data?
Delete items directly in the app, or uninstall Interval to remove all local data from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also turn it off in Settings before deleting.
