Interval is a calm calendar and planning app for capturing what's on your mind, keeping it safe, and deciding what belongs in your day when you're ready. This Privacy Policy explains what information Interval collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Interval does not require an account to use. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information We Collect

Information you create in the app

When you use Interval, you may create and store tasks, notes, attachments, links, and files; calendar placement, reminders, and preferences; and profile settings such as avatar choice and appearance.

This information is stored on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync in Settings, your data is also stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple's iCloud services. We do not receive this data on our own servers.

Information sent to our servers (Pro features only)

If you subscribe to Interval Pro and use certain features, limited data is sent to our servers to provide those features:

  • Refine with AI — When you choose to refine a note, the text content of that note (and related titles) is sent to our backend for processing. Image and file attachments in notes are not sent. We also send an anonymous installation identifier to enforce rate limits. Refined text is returned to your device; we do not use this content for advertising or profiling.
  • Web clipper (Share Extension) — When you share a link to Interval from another app, the URL you share is sent to our backend to generate a title and note summary. We do not access your full browsing history.
  • Siri capture (Pro) — When you add an item via Siri, the dictated text is processed on your device and stored locally (or in your iCloud account if sync is enabled). It is not sent to our servers.

Information handled by Apple

The following are processed by Apple, not by us directly: App Store purchases and subscriptions (via StoreKit), iCloud sync (if enabled), and Apple Calendar access (if you grant permission, events are read on your device to display alongside your tasks; we do not upload your calendar to our servers). We do not receive your payment card number or Apple ID credentials.

Information we do not collect

Interval does not collect your name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact us directly), precise location, contacts, health or fitness data, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics via third-party SDKs.

How We Use Information

We use information only to provide core app functionality, operate Pro features you choose to use, process your subscription through Apple, prevent abuse of our servers, and respond to support requests. We do not use your information for third-party advertising, our own advertising, marketing emails, or cross-app tracking.

Data Storage and Retention

On-device data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app. iCloud data is governed by Apple's iCloud terms and your iCloud settings. Server requests (Refine and web clipper) are processed to fulfill your request; we retain server logs only as long as needed for security, debugging, and abuse prevention, then delete or anonymize them.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate Pro features (cloud hosting and AI processing for Refine, instructed to process data only for the requested feature), with Apple when you use App Store purchases, iCloud, or system services, and if required by law.

Your Choices and Controls

  • Delete your data by deleting items in the app or uninstalling Interval
  • Turn off iCloud sync in Interval Settings
  • Revoke Calendar access in iOS Settings → Interval
  • Avoid server transmission by not using Refine or the web clipper
  • Cancel your subscription in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Contact us at abielievitina@gmail.com with privacy questions or requests

Because Interval has no user accounts, we cannot identify you from server logs unless you contact us and provide details to help us locate a specific request.

Children's Privacy

Interval is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

International Users (including the European Union)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Our legal basis for processing server-side data is performance of a contract and legitimate interests in operating and securing our services. For EU App Store listings, trader contact information may be displayed as required by the Digital Services Act.

California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information sent to our servers, including encrypted connections (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at this URL and change the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Interval after changes means you accept the updated policy.
